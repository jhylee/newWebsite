About Me

Hi, my name is Joseph Lee and I’m a third year co-op student at the Sauder School and the University of British Columbia majoring in Business and Computer Science (BUCS). As an avid technology evangelist and hopeful entrepreneur, the BUCS program has been amazing fit, where I’ve had the opportunity to combine my interests in commerce (accounting, marketing, strategic consulting) with the computer science curriculum (algorithms, data structures, software engineering). After graduation, my ultimate goal is to enter the high-technology industry in a project management or technology consulting role, where I will have the opportunity to utilize a wide array of business and technical skillsets.

Outside of academics, I’ve been in organized/competitive sports for nearly all of my life and as a result, I love playing and watching all forms of sports, especially hockey, football (both kinds), basketball, and of course hitting the links. Unfortunately, it pains me to say that I’m a long-suffering fan of the Senators, Canucks, and Arizona Cardinals (ironic how all three lost in their respective finals)...
